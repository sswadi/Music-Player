The Local Train

18m Followers

Indian Band

The Local Train is an Indian rock band formed in Chandigarh in 2008, and based in New Delhi since 2015. The band's current lineup consists of lead guitarist - Paras Thakur, bassist - Ramit Mehra, and drummer and percussionist - Sahil Sarin. Having started their career by releasing a stream of singles, the band's debut album Aalas Ka Pedh was released in 2015 and their second album Vaaqif followed in 2018. Both the albums have been among the top 5 most-streamed rock music albums on Apple Music India.The band is known for its Hindi and Urdu lyrics, often touching on topics like communalism and faith which has made them popular with younger audiences across India.
